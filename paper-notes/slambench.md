## Proposed Method

- Uses ICL-NUIM dataset

    - depth2vertex
    - vertex2normal
    - track
    - reduce
    - solve (only CPU)
    - integrate
    - raycast
    - renderDepth
    - renderTrack
    - renderVolume


Using RGB-D images measure:
- Frame Rate
- Energy Consumptionk
- absolute trajectory error (ATE)
